Best place to visit in & around Vellore

The city of Vellore has adorned with various historical monuments, religious sites, informative places, and hill stations. Vellor's prime attraction remains its fort but then the city is home to several temples, an observatory and a museum as well.

Vellore Fort

This 16th-century attraction is the heart of Vellore city. Erected by Vijayanagara Empire, this massive fort has historical significance and is one of the prominent places of tourist interests in Vellore. The fort complex includes church, mosque, and temple.

Golden TemLle

Comfortably seated at a temple town called Sripuram, Sri Lakshmi Golden Temple is located at a mere distance of Vellore. The entire structure of the shrine is coated in gold leaf which makes it a phenomenal modern architectural wonder.

VAINU BAPPU OBSERVATORY

Another place of worth visiting in Vellore is Vainu Bappu Observatory. Anyone who wish seek some learning in astronomy, the observatory is the best place for them, especially for kids. Built in a sprawling area of 100 acres, the observatory houses largest telescope of Asia which 93 inches long.

YELAGIRI HHILLS

A tourist place that does not need any introduction! Yelagiri is a hill station in Vellore district which is perched at an elevation of 1110 m above the sea level. As a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu, Yelagiri is enfolded with lush greenery in the form of tea estates and flowery meadows. There is also a lake which is another tourist hotspot in Yelagiri.

AMIRTHI ZOOLOGICAL PARK

An ideal sightseeing place in Vellore is Amirthi Zoological. Located under the Javadhu Hills, the park is spread over a rambling area of 25 hectares. Here, one can witness the exquisite beauty of the hills in its luxuriant vegetation, azure waterfalls, and enchanting wildlife

SCIENCE PARK

Located in the southern region of the Vellore City, Science Park is a popular kid-friendly attraction in the region. Also known as the Vellore District Science Centre, the park has various galleries in the display which include physical science, leather, and environment. For children, the park also organizes summer camps where they teach on various topics.

GOVERMENT MUSEUM

Government Museum is another informative tourist site in Vellore which is located in the fort premises. This multipurpose museum exhibits a great assortment of numerous artefacts of history, botany, geology, and archaeology. In total, the museum has 8 galleries.

DELHI GATE

One of the most revered historical monuments in Vellore, Delhi Gate was once served as the entrance gate to enter into an 18th-century fort built by Daulat Khan Pann - a governor under Mughal Empire. Not just this, this historical site has also witnessed many great battles, Siege of Arcot is just of them.

RATANAGIRI TEMPLE

Here, one can spot a large number of devotees commemorating and paying homage to Lord Murugan, to whom the temple devoted. Ratnagiri Temple is one of the oldest temples in Tamil Nadu that traces its history from the 14th-century.

ROMAN CATHOLIC DIOCESE

Just at a distance of around 3 km from Vellore Town Railway Station, there is located pristine Christian pilgrimage site - Vellore Diocese, also known as Roman Catholic Diocese. The appealing architectural design and the spiritual ambiance of the church makes it one the must-see attractions in Vellore.
